Welcome to SAUS

The Shaikh Ayaz University, formerly Shikarpur Campus of Shah Abdul Latif University Khairpur, came into being with the December 12 notification of Government of Sindh to this effect. The University is named after widely known poet of Sindh Shaikh Ayaz who belonged to Shikarpur city.
